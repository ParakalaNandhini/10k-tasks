CARTOON BAND

We love music.

Cartoon is an indie-electronic trio that blends smooth beats, soulful vocals, and atmospheric vibes. With influences ranging from synth-pop to R&B, their sound is a journey through the hazy landscapes of emotion. Lead vocalist Phoebe's haunting voice weaves seamlessly with the electronic beats crafted by Pulse and Rhythm, the dynamic duo on synths and drums. Together, they create an immersive experience, inviting you to get lost in the dreamy allure of cartoon's sonic universe.
