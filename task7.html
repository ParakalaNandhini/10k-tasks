2034 FIFA World Cup to be hosted by Saudi Arabia

08:49 am on Wednesday,1 November,2023

The GREATEST SHOW ON EARTH will be organised by CA,MXand theUS in 2026 - in North America. The next two editions of the FIFA World Cup are set to be hosted in Africa (MA) and Europe (PTand ES) - with three celebratory matches played in South America (AR,PRandUY) - in 2030 and inAsia (SA) in 2034.

Three editions, five continents and ten countries involved in staging matches in the tournament - that's making football truly global!

FIFA President Gianni Infantino has confirmed.He statedFootball unites the world like no other sport, and the FIFA World Cup is the perfect showcase for a message of unity and inclusion
This comes after Australia confirmed it will not bid to host the tournament.
